About Us

Located in Ilwaco, Washington, Moses Fishing Guide has proven to be the best guide service in the Pacific Northwest. Captain Moses and his crew have fished these waters for 20+ years and know them better than anyone around. We offer a variety of fishing trips to choose from, stretching from as far south as the Columbia River to as far north as Puget Sound.

Freshwater Fishing

We offer freshwater fishing trips along the Columbia River, starting at the mouth upriver to the Snake River confluence. We chase chinook (king) salmon, coho salmon, and sturgeon (pictured).

Saltwater Fishing

From the mouth of the Columbia River up to Puget Sound, we chase halibut (pictured), dungenous crab, sea bass, lingcod, chinook (king) salmon, and coho salmon.
